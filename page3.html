CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Chrisostomo Ibarra
  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, also known as Crisostomo Ibarra
  • He studied in Europe for 7 years/li>
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara, whose full name was Maria Clara de los Santos, was the fiancée of Crisostomo Ibarra and the daughter of Capitan Tiago.
Elias
  • Elias was a wanted man who resided in San Diego. After experiencing tragedy after catastrophe, he started trying to make society better.
  • Crisostomo Ibarra helped the young man and repeatedly saved him after the young man saved his life.
Kapitan Tiago
  • Maria Clara's father, Capitan Tiago, was a prominent businessman in San Diego. His daughter was wed to Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Capitan Tiago was a submissive and modest man.
Padre Damaso
  • The real father of Maria Clara was Padre Damaso, a former town curate in San Diego.
  • According to reports, Padre Dámaso is an extrovert who is haughty, merciless, and judgmental.
Padre Salvi
  • Padre Salvi uses his position as a priest to exert political influence, which is advantageous to both himself and the church.
  • Padre Salvi succeeded Padre Damaso as the town curate of San Diego.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Crisstomo Ibarra's father is Don Rafael.
  • He was the wealthiest guy in San Diego, but he was also the most moral and giving.
Don Anastasio
  • Anastacio, also known as Pilosopo Tasio, was an elderly scholar who resided in San Diego. /li>
  • Once studied philosophy and favors secular education above Catholicism.

Sisa
  • Sisa was the mother of Basilio and Crispin and a resident of San Diego.
  • Sisa went wild after both of her sons vanished and wandered the town looking for them.
Crispin
  • Along with his brother Basilio, Crispin served as a sacristan for the church while he was a young boy living in San Diego.
  • Crispin received a punishment from the head sacristan and Padre Salvi after being suspected of robbing the church of funds.
Basilio
  • Basilio, Crispin's older brother, was a small child living in San Diego.
  • Basilio left the village after learning of his mother's passing and his brother's disappearance, and Capitan Tiago took him in.
Dona Victorina
  • Doa Victorina was a well-known socialite and Capitan Tiago's companion.
  • She and her husband Don Tiburcio aimed to network with San Diego's powerful people in order to elevate their social status.
Don Tirburcio
  • In Jose Rizal's Noli Me Tangere, Doa Victorina's hen-pecked husband was Don Tiburcio de Espadaa.
  • Being a proud Spanish doctor in the Philippines was on his wife's agenda.
Donya Consolation
  • a senior Filipina who is the ensign's wife.
Alperes
  • Alperes is the head of the Civil Guard.
Tiya Isabel
  • Maria Clara is primarily looked after by her aunt, Isabel Alba.
